About Me

I am the sole child of my deceased parents. As an independent government contractor, I am the owner of my own Telecom/Petroleum Company called MC Ventures, which has been in existence since March 10, 2010. I was born into a humble Christian household, with my mother hailing from Chicago and my father originally from Ankara, Turkey. During my upbringing, my visits to the United States were only limited to holiday periods. However, after obtaining a degree from the University of Istanbul, Turkey, I relocated to the USA. Unfortunately, I became a widower when my wife passed away from cancer six years ago. I am blessed with a wonderful daughter named Lucy. I possess properties in both Illinois United States and Turkey, and due to the nature of my profession, I have had the opportunity to travel extensively. In my free time, you can often find me engaged in activities such as writing, reading, cycling, immersing myself in nature, pursuing photography, or spending quality time with my daughter Lucy.
